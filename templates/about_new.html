Our Story

Analytics Pro was built as a comprehensive web analytics solution that combines the power of custom dashboards with Google Analytics integration. Our Flask-based platform provides real-time insights into website performance, user behavior, and engagement metrics.

We believe in making web analytics accessible and actionable for businesses of all sizes. Our platform offers both custom analytics tracking and professional Google Analytics integration to give you complete visibility into your website's performance.
